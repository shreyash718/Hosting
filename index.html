🎯 Candidate Experience

1. Starting the Interview

Enter the interview lobby and verify your details. Ensure you are in a quiet environment for the best voice experience.

2. Enabling Microphone

When the interview starts, the browser will ask for microphone permissions. Click "Allow" to enable the voice-driven interview flow.

3. The Workspace

On the left, you'll see the problem description. In the center is the Monaco Editor, and on the right is your live chat with Arjun.

💼 Recruiter Dashboard

1. Assessment Management

Create assessments by picking problems from our extensive library or adding your own custom challenges.

2. Reviewing Results

Access the dashboard to see all candidate attempts. Each report includes the full code history, chat transcript, and an AI-generated breakdown.

⌨️ Master the Shortcuts

Ctrl + L Toggle Voice Recording (Start/Stop)
Enter Send message to Arjun
Tab Indent code (handled inside editor)

💡 Pro Tips

Voice Not Working?

If Arjun isn't hearing you, refresh the page and ensure the "🎤" icon at the top is turned ON.

Cursor Issues?

If the cursor feels offset, ensure your browser zoom is set to 100% for the best layout accuracy.
